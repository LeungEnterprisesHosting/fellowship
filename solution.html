Solution


Our solution consists of a study hall programs that gives students more autonomy.

As shown in our page on independent study, there are a plethora of benefits and ways education can be improved with implementation in high schools. In our solution, students would be allowed to conduct their own independent studies with no restrictions from the teacher (ex. silence) during study hall class time. Students would be able to focus on their passions.

The second aspect of our solution is that students would also be allowed to leave campus during the study hall period and go wherever they please both on or off campus to give them a greater control over their environment. As explained in our video, leaving the open campus privilege exclusive to higher achieving students would prevent any irresponsible students from taking advantage of a greater level of autonomy that others could benefit from.
