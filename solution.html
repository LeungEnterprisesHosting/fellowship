Solution


Our solution consists of a study hall programs that ultimately gives students more autonomy. Students would be allowed to conduct their own independent studies with no restrictions from the teacher (ex. silence) during study hall class time. Students would also be allowed to leave campus during the period and go wherever they please both on or off campus to give them a greater control over their environment.
